# 羅生門：闇視 肖像生成詠唱集（Image Prompt Library）

本計画における「墨絵ダークファンタジー」の世界観を維持するためのプロンプト記録である。
鳥山石燕の『今昔画図続百鬼』を指針とし、和紙の質感と墨の濃淡、掠れを重視する。

## 🏹 隊員（Allies）

### 1. 渡辺 綱（Watanabe Tsuna）
- **詠唱（Prompt）**: `Sumi-e style character portrait of a powerful samurai Watanabe no Tsuna, dark fantasy, wearing traditional samurai armor with a long katana, determined and fierce expression, monochromatic with sharp brush strokes, parchment paper background, high contrast portrait.`

### 2. 安倍 晴明（Abe Seimei）
- **詠唱（Prompt）**: `Sumi-e style character portrait of an Onmyoji (diviner) Abe no Seimei, dark fantasy, holding a folding fan, mystical and calm expression, wearing traditional Heian period court noble robes (Kariginu), monochromatic with subtle ink wash effects, parchment paper background, high contrast portrait.`

### 3. 八百比丘尼（Yaobikuni）
- **詠唱（Prompt）**: `Sumi-e style character portrait of a Buddhist nun Yaobikuni, dark fantasy, ethereal and immortal look, eyes that have seen centuries, wearing traditional white and grey monk robes, monochromatic with subtle green accents like fresh leaves, holding a wooden staff, parchment paper background, high contrast portrait.`

## 👻 怪異（Enemies）- 百鬼夜行絵巻（Sekien Style）

すべての怪異は、鳥山石燕の筆致を模した「日本画・墨絵」様式で統一されている。

- **共通基盤**: `Japanese ukiyo-e style illustration of [ENEMY], inspired by Toriyama Sekien's Konjaku Gazu Zoku Hyakki, Japanese dark fantasy, ink on aged washi paper, monochromatic sumi-e with fine line work, eerie and mystical atmosphere, high contrast.`

### 主要怪異一覧
1. **餓鬼（Gaki）**: `thin emaciated body with a distended belly, eerie and pathetic.`
2. **骸骨（Gaikotsu）**: `large skeletal figure emerging from darkness, inspired by Sekien and Kuniyoshi.`
3. **鉄鼠（Tessatsu）**: `monstrous rat in Buddhist robes with sharp iron teeth.`
4. **人魂（Hitodama）**: `floating ghostly spirit flames/souls with faint spirit faces inside, haunting and beautiful.`
5. **輪入道（Wanyudo）**: `flaming ox-cart wheel with a giant monk's head in the center, terrifying face.`
6. **濡れ女（Nure-onna）**: `wet woman with the body of a snake and long black hair, unsettling.`
7. **青坊主（Ao-bozu）**: `giant blue monk monster with one eye or hidden face, colossal and mysterious.`
8. **土蜘蛛（Tsuchigumo）**: `giant demon spider with multiple eyes and human-like legs, terrifying.`
9. **牛鬼（Ushi-oni）**: `giant cow-headed demon spider with bovine features, massive and terrifying.`
10. **鵺（Nue） - BOSS**: `chimera with the head of a monkey, body of a tanuki, tiger limbs, and snake tail, dark storm clouds.`

## 🎨 世界観維持のための心得
1. **構成**: 常時 `Japanese ukiyo-e style` と `Toriyama Sekien inspired` を指定せよ。
2. **色彩**: `Monochromatic` (単色) を基本とし、和紙（`aged washi paper`）の質感を背景に置け。
3. **筆致**: `Fine line work` (細密な線) と `Ink wash` (墨の濃淡) の対比を強調せよ。
